Background
A retail user suggested adding a dedicated stock list showing stocks currently within the recommended buy price range, making it easier to monitor opportunities without manually checking each stock.
This feedback reflects a common scenario among retail investors. During volatile or declining markets, users tend to become less confident and prefer more intuitive guidance on which stocks are currently actionable, rather than interpreting chart patterns individually.
Currently, MarketSmith already supports identifying these stocks through existing pattern-related factors and chart buy zones. However, the entry point may not be sufficiently discoverable for retail users.
Therefore, we would like to evaluate whether the current experience can be further optimized.
